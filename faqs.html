Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visa requirements for visiting Taniti?

Tourists need a valid passport and may require a visa depending on the country of origin.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Yes, Taniti is known for being a safe and welcoming destination.

What is the best time to visit Taniti?

The best time to visit Taniti is between December and April when the weather is most favorable.

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar (USD), and most major credit cards are accepted.

Is Wi-Fi available on the island?

Yes, most hotels, resorts, and cafes offer free Wi-Fi. Some remote areas may have limited connectivity.

Are there medical facilities on the island?

Yes, there are clinics and a hospital in the main town. For emergencies, medical evacuation is available.
